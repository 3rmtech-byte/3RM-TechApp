# SETUP.md

## Step-by-Step Instructions for Setting Up MIT App Inventor

### 1. Setting Up MIT App Inventor
   - Go to the [MIT App Inventor website](http://appinventor.mit.edu).
   - Click on "Create Apps!" to sign in with your Google account.
   - If it's your first time, you may need to complete some initial setup.

### 2. Creating the First Screen
   - Once logged in, click on "Projects" in the top menu bar.
   - Select "Start a New Project" and give your project a name.
   - Click "OK" to create the new project.

### 3. Adding Components
   - On the left panel, you will see the Palette with various components.
   - Drag and drop components (e.g., Buttons, Labels) onto the Screen.
   - To set properties for each component, click on the component on the viewer and modify the properties on the right panel.
   
### 4. Exporting the Android APK
   - Once you are satisfied with your app, click on the "Build" button at the top.
   - Choose "App (provide QR code for .apk)" or "App (save .apk to my computer)".
   - Follow the on-screen instructions to download your APK.

### Conclusion
   - You can now install the APK on your Android device and run your app!
